About Girivar Bhawan

Girivar Bhawan (Hostel 7) is one of the premier boys' hostels at NIT Kurukshetra. It provides a comfortable and conducive environment for students to pursue their academic and personal development. With world-class facilities and a vibrant community, Girivar Bhawan is home to 352 students.
