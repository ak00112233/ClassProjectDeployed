Leave Policy

  • Medical Leave: Valid medical certificate required from government hospital.
  • Emergency Leave: Must be approved by Hostel Warden within 24 hours.
  • Casual Leave: Maximum 5 days per semester without reason. Notice required.
  • Vacation: During official holidays and semester breaks. No permission required.
  • Application Deadline: Submit leave request at least 2 days before departure.
  • Approval Time: Usually approved within 24-48 hours.
